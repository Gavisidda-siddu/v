How do I book an appointment? +
Simply navigate to the "Book Appointment" page, fill in your details, select your preferred department, date, and time, then submit the form.
How does video consultation work? +
Video consultations allow you to connect with doctors remotely. After booking a consultation slot, you'll receive a link to join the video session at the scheduled time.
How do I track my medications? +
Go to the Medication Tracker page and add your medications with dosage and reminder times. The system will help you manage your medication schedule.
Can I access my medical records? +
Yes, you can view your medical records, lab results, and appointment history on the Medical Records page.
What should I do in an emergency? +
In case of an emergency, click the SOS button on the Emergency SOS page or call 911 immediately. The SOS feature will show you emergency contacts.
How do I find blood donors? +
Visit the Blood Bank page to search for nearby blood banks and available donors. You can search by location and blood type.
Is my personal information secure? +
Yes, we take data security seriously. All your personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely according to healthcare privacy standards.
Can I request a callback from the hospital? +
Absolutely! Visit the "Request Callback" page, provide your contact information and reason, and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
